inspirational websites

Daniel Spatzek Portfolio

I chose this website as inspiration because it is so fun and crazy and whimiscal. Daniel Spatzek really had a fun time messing around with his portfolio. Also this animated banana is hilarious.

Babylone Website

I am inspired by this website because I really enjoy the color pallete, moving static-like texture, and marquees. The condensed fonts are very nice and the constant movement throughout is pleasing. I also am inspired by the thin black strokes and wide fonts.

Flying Papers Website

I am inspired by this website because of the moving type. The stretching and squishing of the words is so fun and eye-catching. There are bright colors with white text. Also the animations are my favorite.
